# Project: The Real One (Настоящий) ## Overview Third-person 3D city exploration game for mobile. **Core Vibe:** Freedom, friendship, nostalgic early 2000s-2010s urban aesthetic. Light, warm, and hopeful. ## Aesthetic & Art Style * **Style:** Cartoonish, "Subway Surfers" proportions (slightly oversized heads, strong silhouettes). * **Inspiration:** Subway Surfers, Jet Set Radio, GTA Chinatown Wars. * **Setting:** Stylized realistic city. Apartment blocks, rooftops, abandoned buildings, neon signs, graffiti. * **Lighting:** Warm orange evening light, starry nights with city glow. * **Soundtrack:** Lo-fi hip-hop, acoustic guitar, ambient city sounds. ## Characters ### Kit (Protagonist) * **Age:** 12 years old. * **Appearance:** Light fluffy curly hair (copper/wheat), hoodies, t-shirts, bandages on hands/shins, high-top sneakers. * **Personality:** Sunny, positive, always laughing. * **Calling Card:** His graffiti tag "Kit" found throughout the city. ### NPCs * **Archi (Mentor):** 25, wanderer, sits on rooftops drinking tea. Guitar on back, red bandana, leather jacket, wise and calm. * **Derzey (First Friend):** Energetic, bold, always wears a gas mask. ## Gameplay Modes 1. **RUN Mode:** Endless runner/parkour. Camera behind Kit. Speed increases, swipes/taps for jumping, sliding, wall-running. 2. **EXPLORE Mode:** Free roam. joystick movement. Enter buildings, find secrets, talk to NPCs (short, contextual dialogues). ## Key Locations * City blocks (shops, school). * Abandoned building (favorite spot, dark corridors, great rooftop view). * Meadow (secret spot outside the city for stargazing). * Interconnected rooftops. ## Interaction Design * **Mobile-First:** Touch controls (joystick for Explore, swipes for Run). * **Camera:** Third-person, follows Kit. Dynamic zoom based on mode. * **Dialogues:** Short, pop-up style (GTA-like), non-intrusive.
